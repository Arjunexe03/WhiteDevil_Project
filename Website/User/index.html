Report your problem .

Citizens can easily submit their grievances through the system by providing necessary details about their issues. The AI-powered platform ensures seamless data collection and verifies the completeness of the information. This step helps in gathering structured complaints, making it easier for authorities to process them efficiently.

Categorises your problem .

Once a complaint is submitted, the AI analyzes the grievance and categorizes it into predefined sectors such as infrastructure, public services, or law enforcement. This automated classification helps authorities quickly identify common patterns, prioritize urgent cases, and allocate resources effectively for faster resolution.

Help in finding solution fast .

After categorization, the system suggests possible solutions based on past cases and assigns complaints to the relevant department for action. Users can track the status of their grievances in real time, while authorities get insights to improve redressal mechanisms. This step ensures a data-driven, efficient, and transparent resolution process.
